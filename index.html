Welcome to Carillion Guitars - high end custom built guitars designed for serious players who demand quality materials, precision and the ability to add their own personal unique flair to their instrument. Each guitar is handmade from inception to completion in our workshop in Surrey, England and are shipped worldwide.

We are proud to have supplied instruments to members of prolific bands such as Slipknot, Nile, Aborted, Cytotoxin, Viscera and many others.


On this page you can keep up with the latest Carillion Guitars news, view our latest build galleries and also place enquiries for your own custom build project.

Chris Delia - Owner and Chief Luthier.

Viscera announce tour w/TBDM and RoS

Viscera announce tour w/TBDM and RoS

The band have announced the ‘Verminous European Tour 2021‘ is coming next January. It sees the band performing a massive 42 shows around Europe and the UK. The tour will kick off on 6th January in Essen, and finish 20th February in Stuttgart. They will be joined by rising acts Rings of Saturn and Viscera throughout the whole tour.
